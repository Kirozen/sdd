Invoke the **sdd-research** skill (`skills/sdd-research/SKILL.md`). Treat
`$ARGUMENTS` as the unknown to research. Prefer primary sources, distill each
answer to one terse line + its source, and persist via `sdd add-research`.
Unsourced claims are flagged `?`, never written as fact.
