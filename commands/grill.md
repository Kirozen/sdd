---
description: Sharpen a fuzzy idea into a feature's goal + constraints before any task exists. One question at a time, recommended answers, unknowns parked.
argument-hint: [idea | "grill me"]
---

Invoke the **sdd-grill** skill (`skills/sdd-grill/SKILL.md`). Treat `$ARGUMENTS`
as the idea to interrogate. Calibrate pressure, climb the question ladder one
question at a time with a recommended answer each rung, and persist settled
answers into spec.db via the `sdd` CLI. Park unresolved unknowns as `?` items —
never guess a constraint. Hand the sharpened goal/constraints to the spec skill.
